## AI Automation Workflow Diagram
A professional, interactive diagram showcasing an AI-powered business automation workflow. Built with HTML and CSS, featuring:
- Clean, modern design with a dark background and teal accents
- Clearly defined steps with icons, titles, and descriptions
- Animated connecting arrows to visualize the workflow
- Colored badges indicating the tools used in each step
- Key statistics highlighting the benefits of the automation
- Fully responsive layout for seamless viewing on any device
